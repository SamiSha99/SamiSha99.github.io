FrostBurn Cliffs:

It's considered my "first big mod" project, a mod that tries to add environmental mechanics which gives speed, a jump boost and powers focused around ice or fire.

The level is fast paced so the player suppose to utilize these mechanics to overcome the level as fast as possible.

During the development I've made a small demo which got a lot of feedback, both positive and negative, the feedback has helped me in improving the main mod itself.
